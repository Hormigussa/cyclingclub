    The Silver riders cycling club is a cycling club founded in the year 1966 in North eastern England with the purpose of bringing together old riders and take part in regular gentle exercise, Although all kinds of cyclists are catered for it is the older generation who attend most of the events and rides


     Due to an increase of the ageing population the number of members has also risen, however this doesnt mean we discourage new members, infact we encourage everyone to come join us and spread word about us.


     At Silver Riders Cycling Club we are open one evening per week and on both Saturday and Sunday weekends, on top of that we organise events and rides for a range of abilities, covering different routes and difficulty of rides altogether. The club has a series of events and there are 3 different groups to choose from
